YEN'S EGG AVOCADO RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread(toasted)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt, pepper and red pepper flakes(to taste)

Procedure:

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Mash the avocado unto the toast and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook eggs to preference (fired or scrambled) and place on toop of the avocado.
  4. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
